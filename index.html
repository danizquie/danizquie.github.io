Welcome to International Pharmacy

Located in Hialeah, Florida, International Pharmacy is a full-service pharmacy committed to providing high-quality care and service to our customers. Our experienced staff is here to help you with all of your pharmacy needs, including prescription filling, medication management, and over-the-counter products. We accept a variety of insurance plans and offer delivery options for your convenience. Thank you for choosing International Pharmacy!

About Us

International Pharmacy was founded in 2010 by John and Jane Smith. Our goal has always been to provide top-notch service and care to our customers. Our team consists of experienced pharmacists, technicians, and support staff who are dedicated to helping you achieve optimal health. We are proud to be accredited by the National Association of Boards of Pharmacy and to hold a 5-star rating from the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services. Thank you for choosing International Pharmacy!
